# 📊 E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

The E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Data Analytics project designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, profitability, and regional trends. The project combines Python-based Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) with interactive Power BI dashboards to generate actionable business insights.

This project demonstrates the complete data analytics workflow, including data cleaning, preprocessing, visualization, dashboard development, and business reporting.

## Key Business Insights

### Sales Insights

- Technology category generated the highest overall sales revenue.
- Office Supplies maintained consistent sales performance throughout the year.
- Sales peaked during promotional and festive periods.

### Profitability Insights

- Technology products contributed the highest profit margins.
- Some products showed high sales volume but low profitability due to discounting.

### Customer Insights

- A small percentage of customers contributed a large share of total revenue.
- Corporate and Consumer segments generated the highest sales volume.

### Regional Insights

- West region recorded the highest sales performance.
- Certain regions exhibited strong sales but lower profit margins.

### Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-profit categories.
- Reduce unnecessary discounting on best-selling products.
- Implement loyalty programs for top customers.
- Increase inventory availability in high-performing regions.
